## Format

Rules follow the numbering hierarchy of the official document:

```
# GR – General Regulations          - section (H1)
## GR.1 Competition Objective       - major subsection (H2)
### GR.1.1 Collegiate Design Series - subsection (H3)
#### GR.1.4.1 Teams will act as…    - individual rule (H4)
```

Rule IDs referenced in body text are linked to their definitions, including cross-file links (e.g. a rule in **EV** linking to **F**).
